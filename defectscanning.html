Barcode Scanner App

In a team of 3, we are tasked to design and develope an app that is able to scan and display the number represented by the barcode. Throughout this project, we have sucessfully develope the app. Additionally, we have implemented a checksum system that checks if the number displayed is correct. We have also modified the app to scan the barcode even if it is upside down.
