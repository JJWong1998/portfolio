Automatic Drilling Machine

In a team of 3, we are tasked to propose, design and develope a machine that is able to undergo everyday task automatically. Throughout this project, we sucessfully built a machine that is capable of drilling a hole in thecentre of a 5 x 5 x 2cm wooden block. Additionally, a quality checking station was added to ensure that the hole was drilled at the centre. A sorting system was implemented to removes the blocks that fails the quality check.
