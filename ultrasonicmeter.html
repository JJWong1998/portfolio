Ultrasonic Distance Meter

In a team of 2, we have sucesfully develope an ultrasonic distance meter that is able to measssure the distance of an object from 3cm to 30cm. Additionally, the device is able to meassure the distance of an object in both imperial(inches) and metric(cm) unit with a 7 ssegment display displaying the mode.
